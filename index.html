About Me

Hi! I’m Gloria, a Computer Engineering student at the University of Birmingham with a passion for building smart systems and solving real world problems through tech.

I’m currently working on projects using ESP32, OLED displays, sensors, and microcontrollers — including an AI powered accountability assistant with a personality, voice feedback, and interactive features. I’m also exploring Raspberry Pi and AI integration to push the boundaries of what I can build.

Long term, I’m working toward launching my own tech/engineering company. I’m especially passionate about innovation that empowers communities and creates lasting impact, particularly in underrepresented regions like Africa.

I’m here to connect with engineers, founders, and creatives who are building the future. Let’s connect. Let’s create.
